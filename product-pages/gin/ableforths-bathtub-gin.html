Ableforth's Bathtub Gin Cask Aged

English Gin

50 cl / 43.3 %

Winner of Best Cask Gin at the World Gin Awards 2016, this tempting tipple from Ableforth’s is a masterclass in spice. It takes all the distillery’s typical vibrancy of orange zest and candied peel, vanilla and a whip-crack of coriander and enriches it with a thick and smooth buttery undertow. A touch of ripe red fruit and a whiff of fudge lift the end to a light, aromatic climax.
